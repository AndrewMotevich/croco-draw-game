About Croco Draw app

Croco-draw is an simple real-time multiplayer game. This game is for two people.

The goal of the first player is make a riddle-word and try to draw this word.

Second player must guess the word.


How to play

  1. Create room or connect to room if you know credentials
  2. Send credentials to your friend or open app in new tab to simulate new user and connect to your room
  3. Click ready button and wait your fiend
  4. For HOST: Write a riddle word and draw your picture
  5. For CLIENT: Try to guess the word and write answer

App Features:

  • Real time drawing with floodFill and other basic tools
  • Rooms for manage games
  • Websocket server with websocket generators
  • Cool design with prime-ng library
  • Integrated NX mono-repository with CI/CD (CI with GithubAction and CD to Vercel.com and Render.com)

Stack of technology's:

  • Angular
  • RxJs
  • Prime-Ng
  • Websocket (client / server)
  • NX workspace and CI/CD
  • ngneat/until-destroy
